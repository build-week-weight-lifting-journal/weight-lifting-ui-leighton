Lambda Lifts

Metricize your goals

A sleek tool to track your weightlifting goals!



No other app on the market specifically targets and measures your progress like Lambda Lifts.

Lambda Lifts works like a daily journal where you can access previous workouts to measure your gains. Not sure if you are improving? Well, simply select an archived workout and compare your new results.

Identify weak-points to rechart your muscle building goals!

Lambda Lifts is unique in that it allows you to create new workouts per muscle group. You can then journal the weight and total reps to compare with archives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a tool that improves the efficiency of goal tracking for weightlifters.
